Quick Start

Start here (60 seconds)

  • Open /login and sign in with a demo role.
  • Admins: confirm Users, Funds, and Assignments are configured.
  • Fund Managers: select a fund in the header, then explore the 7 tabs.
  • Use the Extension to capture investors and review them in Research Capture.

Three modules, one platform

Each guide covers every feature, every workflow, and every action available in that module.

Admin Dashboard

Global governance and visibility across all funds, users, and investors on the platform.

  • Real-time KPI cards + fund health table
  • Create and manage users, funds, assignments
  • Cross-fund investor search and filtering
  • AI-powered duplicate detection and merging
  • Investor Requests from Chrome Extension
  • Platform-wide persona analytics + feedback
Open Admin Guide

Fund Manager Dashboard

Fund-scoped execution across 7 tabs — all data isolated per assigned fund.

  • Capital Overview: live raise metrics + stage funnel
  • Task Manager: auto + manual tasks with priorities
  • Investor Profiles: 4-tab detail panel
  • 13-stage Kanban pipeline with drag-and-drop
  • Gmail inbox, call logs, email templates
  • Research Capture + AI persona scoring (0–100%)
Open FM Guide

Chrome Extension

Capture investor profiles from any webpage and send them straight into the portal.

  • Works on LinkedIn, company pages, and any bio
  • Auto-extracts name, job title, firm, LinkedIn URL
  • Add notes and relationship context before uploading
  • Captures appear in Research Capture + Investor Requests
  • Accept creates investor + opens profile automatically
