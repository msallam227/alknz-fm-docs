Quick start

Start here (60 seconds)

  • Open /login and sign in with a demo role.
  • Admins: confirm Users, Funds, and Assignments are configured.
  • Fund Managers: select a fund in the header, then operate within the 7 tabs.
  • Use the Chrome Extension to capture investors and review them in Research Capture.

Admin Dashboard

Global governance and visibility across all funds, users, and investors.

  • Real-time KPI cards + fund health table
  • Create users, funds, and fund assignments
  • Cross-fund investor search and filtering
  • AI-powered duplicate detection and merging
  • Review Chrome Extension captures (Investor Requests)
  • Platform-wide persona analytics and feedback responses

Fund Manager Dashboard

Fund-scoped execution across 7 tabs — all data isolated per fund.

  • Capital Overview: live raise metrics and stage funnel
  • Task Manager: auto + manual tasks with priorities
  • Investor Profiles: 4-tab detail panel (Identity, Context, Contact, Personas)
  • Fundraising Pipeline: 13-stage Kanban with drag-and-drop
  • Communication Center: Gmail inbox, call logs, email templates
  • Research Capture + AI Persona scoring (0–100%)

Chrome Extension

Capture investor profiles from any webpage and send them to the portal.

  • Works on LinkedIn profiles, company pages, and bios
  • Auto-extracts name, job title, firm, and LinkedIn URL
  • Add notes and relationship context before uploading
  • Captures appear instantly in Research Capture (FM) and Investor Requests (Admin)
  • Accept flow creates the investor and opens their profile automatically
